Welcome to Halušky House!

At Halušky House, we offer an authentic taste of Slovakia with a variety of traditional dishes made with love and passion. Whether you're craving the comforting warmth of halušky, the hearty flavors of pirohy, or the rich, tangy taste of kapustnica, we’ve got something to satisfy every appetite.

Our menu features a mix of starters, mains, and desserts, all prepared with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Come for the food, stay for the experience!

We look forward to serving you and sharing the flavors of Slovakia. Explore our menu, enjoy the cozy atmosphere, and let us make your visit unforgettable.
