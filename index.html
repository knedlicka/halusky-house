Welcome to Halušky House!

At Halušky House, we bring you the authentic taste of Slovakia with our specialty dish: bryndzové halušky.

What makes our bryndzové halušky truly special is the bryndza itself. Bryndza is a traditional Slovak sheep's cheese, known for its unique, rich flavor and smooth texture. At Halušky House, we proudly use bryndza sourced from local farms, ensuring the freshest and highest-quality ingredients while supporting our regional farmers.

Our menu offers a variety of other traditional Slovak dishes, from hearty soups like kapustnica (sauerkraut soup) to sweet treats like parené buchty (steamed buns filled with jam).

Come and enjoy the flavors of Slovakia at Halušky House, where tradition meets quality and every meal feels like home.
